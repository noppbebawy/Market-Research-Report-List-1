<p><strong>The Gene Expression Profiling Analysis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RNA Sequencing</li><li>Real-Time Quantitative PCR</li><li>Digital PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene expression profiling analysis encompasses various market types, including RNA sequencing, real-time quantitative PCR (qPCR), and digital PCR. RNA sequencing provides comprehensive insights into gene expression by analyzing the entire transcriptome, allowing for the detection of novel transcripts and splice variants. Real-time qPCR quantifies specific RNA molecules, offering high sensitivity and precision for targeted gene analysis. Digital PCR, on the other hand, enables absolute quantification of nucleic acids, enhancing sensitivity and reproducibility for rare variant detection in diverse applications.</p></p>
