<p><strong>The Next-Generation Titrator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Titrator</li><li>Coulometric</li><li>Volumetric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Next-Generation Titrator market encompasses three primary types: potentiometric, coulometric, and volumetric titrators. Potentiometric titrators measure the potential difference in a solution to determine endpoint accurately, making them ideal for pH and redox reactions. Coulometric titrators measure the amount of electricity used to react analytes, suitable for trace-level analysis. Volumetric titrators rely on the precise measurement of liquid volume to achieve endpoint determination. Each type caters to specific analytical needs across various industries, enhancing precision and efficiency in chemical analysis.</p></p>
