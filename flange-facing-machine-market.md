<p><ul><li>OD Mounted Flange Facing Machines</li><li>ID Mounted Flange Facing Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flange facing machine market is divided into two main types: OD mounted and ID mounted flange facing machines. OD mounted machines are designed to operate on the outer diameter of flanges, providing precision machining for larger components. They are typically used in heavy industries where robustness is essential. In contrast, ID mounted flange facing machines work on the inner diameter, making them suitable for smaller or intricately designed flanges, offering versatility and precision in tight spaces. Both types enhance flange performance and reliability.</p></p>
