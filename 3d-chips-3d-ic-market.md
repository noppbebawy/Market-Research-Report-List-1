<p><p>3D chips, or 3D integrated circuits (ICs), enhance performance by stacking multiple layers of silicon wafers. The 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP) offers compactness and improved thermal performance by integrating chips at the wafer level. Through-Silicon Via (TSV) technology allows vertical electrical connections between layers, increasing speed and reducing space. Other market types include advanced packaging techniques, hybrid bonding, and fan-out wafer-level packaging, all aiming to optimize performance, efficiency, and miniaturization in electronic devices.</p></p>
