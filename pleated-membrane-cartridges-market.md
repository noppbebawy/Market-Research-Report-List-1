<p><p>The pleated membrane cartridges market consists of various filter types designed for specific applications. PP (Polypropylene) filters are ideal for chemical compatibility and are widely used in water purification. PES (Polyethersulfone) filters offer high flow rates and excellent filtration efficiency, suitable for biotech applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) filters provide superior chemical resistance, making them ideal for aggressive solvents. Nylon filters are known for their durability and are often used in industrial applications. "Others" encompasses alternative materials and hybrid filters, catering to niche market needs.</p></p>
