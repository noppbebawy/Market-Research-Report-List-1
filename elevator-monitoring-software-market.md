<p><p>Elevator monitoring software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software operates over the internet, allowing for remote access, easy updates, and scalability, making it ideal for managing multiple elevators across various locations. On-premise software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring more intensive IT management. Both types aim to enhance elevator performance and safety through real-time monitoring and analytics.</p></p>
