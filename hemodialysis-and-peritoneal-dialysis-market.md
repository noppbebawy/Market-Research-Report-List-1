<p><p>Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis are two primary methods used to treat kidney failure. Hemodialysis involves diverting blood from the body to a machine that filters out waste products and excess fluids before returning the cleaned blood to the body. This process typically takes place in a dialysis center or at home with the help of specialized equipment. </p><p>On the other hand, peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdomen (the peritoneum) as a filter. A sterile solution is introduced into the abdominal cavity, where it absorbs waste products from the blood through the peritoneal membrane before being drained away.</p><p>The Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.20% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising prevalence of chronic kidney disease, advancements in dialysis technologies, and a growing aging population that is more susceptible to renal disorders. Additionally, trends such as home-based dialysis treatments, increased awareness about kidney health, and innovations in biocompatible materials for dialysis solutions are shaping the future of the market, catering to the increasing demand for more personalized, convenient, and effective treatment options.</p></p>
