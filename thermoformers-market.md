<p><p>The thermoformers market is categorized into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual thermoformers require human intervention for each step, making them suitable for small-scale production and custom jobs. Semi-automatic thermoformers streamline some processes while still relying on manual input for others, balancing efficiency and control. Fully automatic thermoformers are designed for high-volume production, featuring advanced technology that minimizes human involvement, increases speed, and ensures consistent quality, making them ideal for large manufacturers and mass production.</p></p>
