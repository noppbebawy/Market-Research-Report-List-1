Electronic systems use advanced sensors and controls to actively manage airflow and humidity levels, offering precise adjustments. Pneumatic systems rely on air pressure to achieve ventilation, providing a simpler and often more cost-effective solution. Electro-pneumatic systems combine both technologies, using electronic controls to optimize pneumatic airflow, delivering a balanced approach that ensures efficient ventilation while maintaining user comfort during sleep.</p></p>
