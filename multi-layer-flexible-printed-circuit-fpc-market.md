<p><p>The Multi-layer Flexible Printed Circuit (FPC) market is categorized into two main types: circuits with adhesive and circuits without adhesive. Circuits with adhesive utilize adhesive materials to bond different layers, enhancing durability and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring strong, long-lasting connections. Conversely, circuits without adhesive rely on mechanical methods or specialized processes for layer bonding, offering greater flexibility and lighter weight, ideal for compact applications where minimizing thickness is crucial, such as in wearable devices.</p></p>
