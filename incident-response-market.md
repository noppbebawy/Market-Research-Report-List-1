<p><p>Incident response markets can be categorized into three types: Remote, On-Site, and Cloud. Remote incident response involves handling security breaches or incidents from a distance, utilizing digital tools to analyze and rectify issues without physical presence. On-Site response necessitates physical intervention, where teams deploy to the affected location to address incidents directly and perform thorough investigations. Lastly, Cloud incident response focuses on cloud-based environments, ensuring that data and applications hosted in the cloud are secured and managed effectively during incidents.</p></p>
