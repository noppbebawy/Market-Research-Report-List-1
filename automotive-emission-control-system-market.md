<p><p>The Automotive Emission Control System Market encompasses several key components critical for reducing harmful vehicle emissions. Oxygen sensors monitor and optimize air-fuel mixtures, while EGR valves recirculate exhaust gases to minimize nitrogen oxides. Catalytic converters facilitate chemical reactions to convert pollutants into less harmful substances. Air pumps introduce fresh air to aid in emission reduction, PCV valves manage crankcase gases, and charcoal canisters capture fuel vapors to prevent them from entering the atmosphere, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
