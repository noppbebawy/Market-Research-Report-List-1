<p><p>Power Take-Off Units (PTOs) are mechanical devices that transfer power from a vehicle's engine to auxiliary equipment, commonly used in agricultural and industrial applications. The market for PTOs includes two main types: semi-permanently mounted and permanently mounted units. Semi-permanently mounted PTOs allow for easy attachment and detachment, providing flexibility for various tasks.
