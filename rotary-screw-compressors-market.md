<p><strong>The Rotary Screw Compressors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-free</li><li>Oil-injected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary screw compressors are categorized into two main types based on lubrication methods: oil-free and oil-injected. Oil-free compressors utilize air bearings, ensuring that compressed air remains uncontaminated, making them ideal for sensitive applications like food processing and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, oil-injected compressors use oil to lubricate and cool the compression process, typically delivering higher efficiency and performance in general industrial applications. Both types cater to different operational needs and environmental considerations in various industries.</p></p>
