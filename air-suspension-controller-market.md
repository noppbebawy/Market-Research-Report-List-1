<p><p>The air suspension controller market is divided into two main types: manual control and electronic control. Manual control systems require the operator to make adjustments manually, relying on physical knobs or levers to modify air pressure and suspension settings. In contrast, electronic control systems utilize sensors and automated software to adjust air suspension based on real-time conditions, enhancing ride comfort and vehicle stability. The choice between these types depends on user preference and desired level of automation and responsiveness.</p></p>
