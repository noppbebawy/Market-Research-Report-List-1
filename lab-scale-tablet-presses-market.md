<p><p>Lab scale tablet presses are essential for pharmaceutical research and development, offering two main types: single-punch and rotary. Single-punch presses are manually operated, suitable for small-scale production and R&D, allowing precise control over compaction and formulation tests. In contrast, rotary presses employ multiple stations to produce tablets efficiently, making them ideal for higher volume production while maintaining consistent quality. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing tablet formulations and processes before large-scale manufacturing.</p></p>
