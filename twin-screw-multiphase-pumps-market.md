<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Twin Screw Multiphase Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-screw Pump (TSP)</li><li>Helicoaxial Pump (HAP)</li><li>Progressing-cavity Pump (PCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The twin screw multiphase pumps market encompasses various pump types catering to the transportation of fluids containing gas, liquid, and solids. Twin-screw pumps (TSP) utilize two intermeshing screws to handle high-viscosity fluids. Helicoaxial pumps (HAP) feature a rotating helical screw that enhances efficiency in transferring multiphase mixtures. Progressing-cavity pumps (PCP) employ a helical rotor within a stator to create a consistent flow, making them ideal for challenging applications. Each type addresses specific operational needs in diverse industries.</p></p>
