<p><p>The mold steel market is categorized into three primary types: Cold Work Die Steel, Hot Work Die Steel, and Plastic Mould Steel. Cold Work Die Steel is used for tools that operate at lower temperatures, offering high wear resistance and hardness. Hot Work Die Steel is designed for high-temperature applications, maintaining strength and toughness under heat. Plastic Mould Steel is specifically formulated for molding plastics, featuring excellent machinability and surface finish, crucial for producing high-quality plastic parts. Each type serves distinct industrial applications.</p></p>
