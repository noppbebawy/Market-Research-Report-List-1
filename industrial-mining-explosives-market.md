<p><p>The industrial mining explosives market features several key types, including ammonium nitrate explosives, ANFO (Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil), and emulsion explosives. Ammonium nitrate explosives, typically in powder form, are highly popular due to their effectiveness and ease of handling. ANFO, a mixture of ammonium nitrate and fuel oil, is widely used for its cost efficiency and simplicity in application. Emulsion explosives, consisting of water-resistant mixtures, offer enhanced performance in wet environments, making them ideal for various mining conditions.</p></p>
