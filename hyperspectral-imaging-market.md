<p><ul><li>Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR)</li><li>Short Wave Infrared (SWIR)</li><li>Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave Infrared (LWIR)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyperspectral imaging market comprises several types based on spectral ranges. Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR) captures light from the visible spectrum up to 1.4 micrometers, ideal for agriculture and mineralogy. Short Wave Infrared (SWIR) extends from 1.4 to 3 micrometers, useful for detecting moisture and material identification. Medium Wave Infrared (MWIR) spans 3 to 5 micrometers, commonly applied in industrial and security applications. Long Wave Infrared (LWIR) ranges from 8 to 14 micrometers, primarily used in thermal imaging. Other categories may include specialized hyperspectral applications.</p></p>
