<p><p>The cocoa market consists of three main types: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor is the liquid form of cocoa solids and cocoa butter, serving as the base for chocolate products. Cocoa butter, extracted from cocoa beans, is prized for its smooth texture and is widely used in chocolate and cosmetic formulations. Cocoa powder, produced by grinding cocoa solids after fat extraction, is used in baking and beverages. Together, they cater to diverse culinary and industrial needs.</p></p>
