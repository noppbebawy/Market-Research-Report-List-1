<p><p>Agricultural mulch films are categorized based on their properties and applications. Clear or transparent films allow light penetration, promoting plant growth while suppressing weeds. Black films block light, effectively controlling weeds and retaining soil moisture. Colored films, like red or blue, can influence plant development by affecting temperature and light absorption. Degradable films, made from biodegradable materials, minimize environmental impact by breaking down after use. Other types may include UV-stabilized or reflective films, tailored for specific agricultural needs.</p></p>
