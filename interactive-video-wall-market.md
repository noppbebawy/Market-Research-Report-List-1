LED (Light Emitting Diode) walls excel in brightness and energy efficiency, ideal for large spaces and outdoor applications. DLP (Digital Light Processing) technology utilizes micro-mirrors for superior image quality and seamless integration, often used in professional settings. Each type caters to specific needs, shaping the overall market landscape.</p></p>
