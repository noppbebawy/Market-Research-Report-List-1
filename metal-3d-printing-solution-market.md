<p><p>The Metal 3D Printing Solutions market comprises several key technologies, including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to fuse metal powders layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high precision. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powder, offering faster build times and the ability to work with high-temperature alloys. Other methods include binder jetting and directed energy deposition, contributing to diverse applications across industries like aerospace and healthcare.</p></p>
