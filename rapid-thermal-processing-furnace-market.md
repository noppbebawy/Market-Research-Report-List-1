<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Processing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) furnaces are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, featuring two primary types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP utilizes high-intensity lamps to quickly heat substrates, offering uniform temperature distribution and rapid cycle times, which is ideal for annealing and oxidation processes. In contrast, laser-based RTP employs focused laser beams to selectively heat specific areas, allowing for precise temperature control and minimal thermal stress on the substrate. Both technologies enhance efficiency and performance in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
