<p><strong>The Magnetic Field Sensors for Automotive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Effect Sensors</li><li>Magnetoresistive Effect Sensors</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic field sensors in the automotive market can be categorized into three main types. Hall Effect Sensors detect magnetic fields and are commonly used for position sensing and current measurement. Magnetoresistive Effect Sensors respond to changes in electrical resistance when exposed to magnetic fields, offering high sensitivity for applications like wheel speed detection. Additionally, "Other" market sensors may include fluxgate sensors and giant magnetoresistive sensors, utilized in specialized applications such as navigation and advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).</p></p>
