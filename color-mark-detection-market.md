<p><strong>The Color Mark Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Fiber Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color mark detection technology is primarily categorized into two market types: photoelectric sensors and fiber amplifiers. Photoelectric sensors utilize light beams to detect color marks on objects, providing reliable, non-contact detection ideal for various industrial applications. Fiber amplifiers, on the other hand, employ optical fibers to transmit light for precise color detection, allowing for flexibility in tight spaces and enabling high-speed production processes. Both technologies enhance automation and accuracy in manufacturing, ensuring quality control in product handling and packaging.</p></p>
