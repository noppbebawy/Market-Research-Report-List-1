<p><p>The salon use hair color and dye market is categorized into three main types: temporary, semi-permanent, and permanent hair dyes. Semi-permanent dyes last longer, typically 4-12 washes, providing a more lasting solution without the commitment of permanent color. Permanent hair dyes chemically alter the hair's structure for long-lasting color that endures until new hair growth occurs, catering to clients seeking enduring transformations.</p></p>
