<p><p>Multi-Chip Modules (MCM) are compact assemblies that house multiple integrated circuits (ICs) in a single package to enhance performance and reduce space. The MCM-L (Laminate) market uses a laminate substrate for better thermal and electrical properties. MCM-D (Dual-Die) combines two ICs for improved functionality and efficiency. MCM-C (Ceramic) utilizes a ceramic substrate for high reliability in demanding environments. These types cater to diverse applications, including telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics, addressing specific performance and packaging needs.</p></p>
