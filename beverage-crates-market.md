<p><p>The beverage crates market is categorized into three main types: nestable, stackable, and collapsible. Nestable crates can be inverted and stacked within one another to save space when not in use. Stackable crates are designed to be securely piled on top of one another, optimizing storage during transport. Collapsible crates offer versatility by being able to fold flat, allowing for efficient storage and transport when empty. Each type serves specific logistical needs in beverage distribution and storage.</p></p>
