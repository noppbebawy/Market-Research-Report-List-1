<p><ul><li>Bow Thruster</li><li>Stern Thruster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed thruster market primarily comprises bow thrusters and stern thrusters, both crucial for enhancing a vessel's maneuverability. Bow thrusters are installed at the front, providing lateral movement and aiding in docking and navigation in tight spaces. Conversely, stern thrusters are positioned at the rear, enhancing control during reversing and coastal maneuvers. Both types are essential in various marine applications, including commercial and recreational vessels, improving safety and operational efficiency through enhanced mobility and precision in handling.</p></p>
