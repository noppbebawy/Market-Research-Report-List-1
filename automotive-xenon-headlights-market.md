<p><p>The automotive xenon headlights market is primarily segmented into low beam and high beam lights. Low beam lights provide focused illumination for nighttime driving, minimizing glare for oncoming traffic while enhancing driver visibility. High beam lights, on the other hand, offer a broader and brighter light range, ideal for unlit roads and open highways. Both types utilize xenon gas, delivering superior brightness and energy efficiency compared to traditional halogen bulbs, thus improving road safety and driving experience.</p></p>
