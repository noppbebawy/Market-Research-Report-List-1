<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Ultrasonic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-screen fingerprint scanning solutions primarily fall into two categories: optical and ultrasonic. Optical scanners capture fingerprints by using light to create a detailed image of the fingerprint pattern. They are generally cost-effective but can be affected by external factors like dirt or moisture. Ultrasonic scanners utilize high-frequency sound waves to penetrate the skin's surface, providing enhanced accuracy and a secure capture even in challenging conditions. This technology is known for its resilience and ability to function through various materials, enhancing device security.</p></p>
