<p><strong>The Implantable Infusion Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piston Type Infusion Pumps</li><li>Creeping Type Infusion Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implantable infusion pumps market includes two main types: piston-type and creeping-type infusion pumps. Piston-type infusion pumps use a motor-driven mechanism to deliver precise medication dosages through a piston system, ensuring controlled release. In contrast, creeping-type infusion pumps utilize a flexible tubing mechanism that gradually delivers medication by leveraging a peristaltic action, providing a steady flow. Both types offer advantages in patient compliance and targeted drug delivery, catering to various medical conditions requiring long-term treatment.</p></p>
